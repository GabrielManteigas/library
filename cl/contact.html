Reach Us

From Calicut Railway Station (22 kms)

By Bus
Take a 10minute walk or get a rickshaw to Palayam bus stand and board a bus to REC / NIT Calicut

By Rickshaw
Get a rickshaw from the Railway Station to NIT Calicut.

By Taxi
You may get a taxi from the railway station to NIT Calicut.

From Calicut Airport
Calicut Airport is located at Karipur, 27 Kms south-east of the city and it is 45 kms from NIT Calicut. You can reach NIT Calicut by taxi from the Calicut Airport.
